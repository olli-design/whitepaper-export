# Typography

## Styling

Mesi uses the **Poppins** font family by Indian Type Foundry across all its digital and physical touchpoints:

<table><thead><tr><th width="262">Class</th><th width="276">Weight</th><th>Notes</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><strong>Main headers (H1)</strong></td><td>Bold</td><td>Default: 48 dp</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Sub headers (H2)</strong></td><td>SemiBold</td><td>Default: 24 dp</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Banner (also used as H3)</strong></td><td>SemiBold</td><td>Default: 20 dp</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Ingress</strong></td><td>Medium</td><td>Default: 24 dp</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Body</strong></td><td>Medium</td><td>Default: 12 dp</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Call-to-action</strong></td><td>Bold</td><td>Default: 16 dp</td></tr></tbody></table>

## Font assets

The Poppins font family can be downloaded for free from [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Poppins).&#x20;
