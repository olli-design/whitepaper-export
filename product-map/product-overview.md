## Ecosystem Pillars

The Mesi platform is divided into four primary pillars, each designed to drive value back to the **\$MESI** token and its participants.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Social Engagement" icon="messages">
    A high-speed interface for Fans and Creators to interact through DMs, Tipping, and Subscriptions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Digital Marketplace" icon="shop">
    The hub for minting, licensing, and trading NFTs, supported by the **Public Media Library**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Financial Layer" icon="vault">
    Integrated **BaaS** (Banking as a Service) including the Mesi Card, BTC Savings, and Micro-Loans.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Creator Chain" icon="diagram-project">
    The underlying PoS infrastructure ensuring transparency, gasless transactions, and decentralized settlement.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The User Journey

Regardless of your role, Mesi is designed to facilitate a "Create-to-Earn" or "Engage-to-Earn" lifestyle.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Onboarding">
    Create a profile using social login. A smart contract wallet is automatically provisioned via **Account Abstraction**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interaction & Creation">
    Engage with content or upload your own. All high-value actions are recorded on the **Settlement Layer**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monetization">
    Earn through subscriptions, licensing, or engagement rewards. Payouts are settled instantly in **\$MESI**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wealth Management">
    Utilize the BaaS layer to spend earnings via the **Mesi Card** or save long-term in the **BTC Vault**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Core Product Values

| Value | Implementation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Trust** | AI-driven **Black Wall** prevents undisclosed AI fraud. |
| **Simplicity** | Gasless transactions remove the barriers of entry to Web3. |
| **Ownership** | All content is hashed on-chain, ensuring immutable provenance. |
| **Liquidity** | Direct on/off-ramps allow for real-world spending of digital assets. |
