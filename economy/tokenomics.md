# Tokenomics

This section outlines the plan for allocating Mesi App tokens ($MESI).&#x20;

The tokens will be shared between:

* Investors
* Ecosystem funds and supporting initiatives
* Internal stakeholders

## Investors

* **Micro Pre-Seed Group**: Allocated 1.0% (1,000,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 4 years with a 9-month lockup following TGE. No TGE unlock. Vesting begins 9 months after TGE, releasing 1/39 of the allocation per month. Token price: $0.0010 per token.
* **Seed A Group**: Receives 10.0% (10,000,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 3 years with a 6-month lockup following TGE. No TGE unlock. Vesting begins 6 months after TGE, releasing 1/30 of the allocation per month. Token price: $0.0020 per token..
* **Seed B Group**: Assigned 14.0% (14,000,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 2 years with a 3-month lockup following TGE. No TGE unlock. Vesting begins 3 months after TGE, releasing 1/21 of the allocation per month. Token price: $0.0030 per token.

## Ecosystem

* **Treasury**: Holds 20.0% (20,000,000,000 tokens). Fully released at TGE and maintained as part of the projectâ€™s strategic reserves. No vesting or lockup applies.
* **Community incentives**: Comprises 9.5% (9,500,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 4 years with no lockup. 5% released at TGE for immediate community rewards, and the remaining 95% released linearly at 1/48 per month.
* **Liquidity**: Allocated 1.5% (1,500,000,000 tokens). Fully released at TGE and used to establish liquidity depth on DEXes and CEXes. No vesting or lockup applies.
* **Airdrop**: Accounts for 10.0% (10,000,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 4 years with a 3-month lockup following TGE. No TGE unlock. Vesting begins 3 months after TGE, releasing 1/45 of the allocation per month, distributed under a discretionary strategy.

## Internal stakeholders

* **Core Team**: Receives 25.0% (25,000,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 4 years with a 12-month lockup following TGE. No TGE unlock. Vesting begins 12 months after TGE, releasing 1/36 of the allocation per month.
* **Strategic partnerships**: Assigned 5.0% (5,000,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 4 years with a 12-month lockup following TGE. No TGE unlock. Vesting begins 12 months after TGE, releasing 1/36 of the allocation per month.
* **Advisors**: Allocated 4.0% (4,000,000,000 tokens). Total vesting period of 4 years with a 12-month lockup following TGE. No TGE unlock. Vesting begins 12 months after TGE, releasing 1/36 of the allocation per month.
